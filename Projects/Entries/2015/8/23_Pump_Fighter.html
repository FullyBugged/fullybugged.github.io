Pump Fighter

 

Pump Fighter is a simple 2 player game made for fun.


Find a human friend or enemy, Choose a side, and Tap the screen the fastest you can to explode the head of your opponent!

Originally created in 18 hours on MsDos during a Demo party / Game Jam back in 1998, we decided to port it to Android to have fun with our friends and family! Done in our spare time, re-using the assets from back then with few improvements here and there, it took us a cumulated time of around 18h again to call it ready to be published!


Completely Free, with no Ads, we're happy to offer you this small game!



Availabe now on Google play

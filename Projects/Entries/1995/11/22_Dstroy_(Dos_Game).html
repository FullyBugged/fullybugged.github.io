Dstroy (Dos Game)

 

Developed by Andre Rieussec,  Nicolas Riviere and Jerome Angelot, with the help of Emmanuel Marty, Dstroy was one of the best game of its kind, available during the early 90’s on Pc.

WIth awesome soudtracks on Gravis Ultrasound, up to 4 simultaneous players with multi direction scrolling at 30 fps on a Dx2-66, and against an IA some still remember and get nightmares about, It was a great game to play alone, with, or against friends.

You can play it from your Internet Browser directly HERE thanks to the Internet Database.

(The original MsDos game is available on Abandonia too)
